Gender Issues

Gender inequality has a significant impact on mental health around the world. Gender inequality has been linked to increased levels of stress, anxiety, depression, and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) in women and persons of all genders. When we box in women and men, that is, when we construct preconceptions about how they should think, feel, and behave, and when we limit their natural skills and genuine selves, we are dealing with gender issues. Men and women may be affected differently by gender issues, but studies suggest that women are more disadvantaged. Gender issues are inter-related and vital to one another. Gender issues may not always be directly relevant to people's lives.
